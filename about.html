Spotisave

What is this?

If you have a Spotify account with over 2 weeks of activity, you should have a playlist called "Discover Weekly" curated by Spotify, based on your taste.
The thing is, the playlist disappears every Monday, no matter how good it was. You can never hear it again... unless you save it.

Simply sign in using Spotify and you can export your Discover Weekly playlist to keep it forever.
